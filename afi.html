Day 1 – The Beginning
A LETTER WRITTEN WITH LOVE
My Dearest,

I was hoping to have a great year, with a new mood… even a little scared of what each month might bring.

And then my birthday came to greet me with a gift not just from my mom, but from God, from pure nature itself.

Something fresh, something alive, something my mind was excited to explore… yet my heart was a little afraid, like I could get lost in it.

But then your eyes found mine—
shining so bright, like a quiet light in the dark, telling me without words that I would be fine.

At first, it was joy… soft and unexpected. And then, slowly it became love.

It’s just four words
to express my feelings…
but they carry everything I have.

You look so bright, like a dove gentle, peaceful, graceful in a way I can’t explain. Tall, like someone I can look up to… and somehow still feel safe with.

You are a place where my heart feels at rest, a comfort where I can finally lie down and just be.

With all my heart,
Forever yours
